A better life with Green Planet

A platform with limitless potential in environmental and climate development.

Green Planet, a digital eco-haven, strives for a greener future by uniting climate advocates, businesses, and individuals in a collective effort to combat environmental challenges. Education, sustainable commerce, and waste management are at the core of this transformative platform. Green planet's mission is to empower users with knowledge, stimulate sustainable economic growth, and promote responsible waste disposal, all while mitigating natural disasters and pollution in Nigeria.


By incorporating the FCMB API, Green planet aims to streamline financial transactions in the sector, bridging payment gaps and encouraging environmental entrepreneurship. Key stakeholders include waste gatherers, NGOs, businesses, sellers, and buyers, all working together to advance climate education and promote recycling. Green planet's roadmap includes community building, a waitlist system, virtual events, partnerships, product demos, and a grand virtual launch, ultimately delivering a platform that highlights recyclable products, provides convenient maps for locating recycling facilities, offers a digital wallet for managing earnings, and fosters a vibrant community of environmental enthusiasts.

Frequently Asked Question

How to create an account?

To create an account, visit the Greenplanet's website and click on the "Sign-Up" or "Create Account" button. Fill in your personal information, including your name, email, and a secure password. Verify your email to activate your account, and you're all set to access the full range of features and engage in our vibrant environmental community. Welcome to Greenplanet!

Is there a collaboration feature with climate experts?

Yes, Greenplanet offers a collaboration feature that allows you to connect and collaborate with climate experts, fostering knowledge sharing and collective action in the fight against climate change. You can engage in discussions, workshops, and initiatives to contribute to a sustainable future. Join our platform and connect with experts who share your passion for environmental change.

Is there a mobile app?

Yes, Greenplanet provides a mobile app, available for download on both iOS and Android platforms. The app offers convenient access to all the platform's features, allowing users to engage with climate initiatives, educational resources, and recycling opportunities on the go. Download the app to stay connected with the green movement wherever you are.

How to link my bank to my wallet?

To link your bank to your wallet, navigate to the "Wallet" section within the Green planet platform. Choose the option to "Link Bank Account" and follow the on-screen instructions to input your bank account details securely. Once verified, your bank account will be successfully linked to your wallet, enabling smooth and secure financial transactions within the platform.
